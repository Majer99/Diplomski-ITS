HISTORY OF "M" COMPANY

2023

Thanks to continuous product improvements and a strong marketing strategy, eM Gaming Tech has become a recognizable brand worldwide by 2023. International success and global recognition have led to increased profitability, making eM Gaming Tech one of the leading companies in the gaming accessories industry.

2022

With a strong online presence, eM Gaming Tech opened a flagship store in a gaming metropolis in 2022, setting a standard in retail for gaming accessories. This store became a gathering place for gaming enthusiasts and a location for customers to test the latest products.

2021

The focus on building a gaming community was crucial in 2021. eM Gaming Tech supported local gaming events and tournaments, strengthening its presence in the gaming community.

2020

In 2020, eM Gaming Tech successfully established partnerships with several well-known gaming teams and streamers. This resulted in an expansion of the product range, including keyboards, mice, and headphones, gradually positioning the brand as a comprehensive provider of gaming accessories.

2019

After a year of research and development, eM Gaming Tech launched its first products in 2019. These products quickly gained popularity due to their high quality, modern design, and affordable prices.

2018

eM Gaming Tech was founded in 2018 by enthusiastic gamers with the aim of revolutionizing the gaming industry. The founders wanted to create a brand similar to Razer, Logitech, and Redragon.
