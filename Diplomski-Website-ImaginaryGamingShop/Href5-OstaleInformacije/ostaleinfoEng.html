Return Policy


Return Period

Customers have the right to return products within 30 days of the purchase date.

Return Conditions

The product must be in its original condition, unopened, and with all original packaging, including instructions and accessories. The product must be unused and free from visible signs of damage or wear.

Return Process

To request a product return, please contact us by phone or email to provide you with additional information and instructions. Our support team will provide you with the necessary instructions for returning the product. Customers are responsible for the cost of returning the product.

Verification and Refund

After we receive the returned product and determine that it meets the return conditions, we will process the refund to your account. The refund will be processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned product.

Return Rejection

We reserve the right to reject the return of the product if it is determined that the product does not meet the return conditions.

Warranty and Damaged Products

If the product you received has defects or is damaged, please contact us immediately to provide you with support and resolve the issue in accordance with warranty terms.

Additional Information

For additional information on the return policy or to submit a return request, please feel free to contact us.
